About Us!

What are we about? We're all about the design, feel, the Northeast.

We Design

We are a design company at heart. We will take your ideas and produce gold. Haven’t got any ideas? We can sit with you and come up with them. We strive to take your brand further with our hard-working team and customer service.

We Create

Already have a design? Already have an idea? We can sit with you and create what you want. We can take your ideas and sprinkle them with the Ind.Feat. gold. We enjoy our clients input and love to express what you want, as fun as you want it to be.

We are Local

All our team grew up in the North East. We respect the culture, the people, the industrial heritage that made us who we are. We are here for you from initial contact to delivery, you can rely on us.

Our Story!

Our moto is ”keep our design local”. We want to enhance our area and culture by working with local talent to design anything that is desired, and we have fun doing it.
